Controls

Gesture Control Mode

To activate the MYO-control, a synchronization gesture has to executed. For MYO to react to any future inputs, the gesture Double Tap has to be executed. The color of the circle in the middle of the App should now change from red to yellow.

To completely assume control, either of the two gestures Fist or Finger Spread has to be executed. If the activation is successful, the color of the circle will change from yellow to green.

To release control over the robot, either press the "Stop" button in the App, or execute the gesture Wave In (in which the wrist is tilted inwards). The color of the circle will change from green to red.

IMPORTANT!
The gesture recognition isn't completely accurate yet. Gestures may be misinterpreted or not recognized at all.
If that's the case, it might help to take the bracelet off for a few seconds. After putting it back on, the synchronization gesture has to be executed again.

Touch Control Mode

Touch control is very simple. The left joystick controls the speed, the right one controls the direction of the robot.

Communication

The Smartphone sends relevant data to the robot up to ten times each second.

The control-data is structured as follows:

Command Meaning
ST;

status signal
<L-Value>:<M-Value>;

Direction-Value and Motor-Value are between -100 and 100, the value 0 standing for "driving straight" or "standing still".
